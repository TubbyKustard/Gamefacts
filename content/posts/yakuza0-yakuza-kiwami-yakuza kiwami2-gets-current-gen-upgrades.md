Announced during RGG’s Direct, Yakuza 0: Director’s Cut is coming to current-gen consoles. This edition introduces a brand-new English dub and a four-player online raid mode called Red Light Raid.

As for Kiwami and Kiwami 2, the re-releases don’t include new features beyond current-gen optimization. Additionally, players who own the last-gen versions won’t receive a free or discounted upgrade.
